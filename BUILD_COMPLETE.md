## ğŸ¨ PHILOSOPHY

### "Behavior is structure. Structure is style. Style is logic."

ScrollForge unifies three traditionally separate concerns into one reactive graph:

1. **Events** trigger **Actions**
2. **Actions** mutate **Signals**
3. **Signals** update **Components**
4. **Signals** update **Styles**
5. Everything stays in sync automatically

### Shared Variables Theory

The core insight: **Connect everything through shared global state.**

A single dispatcher routes all actions. Global signals connect all parts of the system. This is the "apex manager" pattern.
